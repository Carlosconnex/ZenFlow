## Communication Plan

- Primary communication: Discord / Slack / Canvas Messages
- Team check-ins: As needed or at least once per week
- All major updates are communicated through GitHub issues and pull requests

## Workflow & Git Practices

- Feature branches are used for all development work
- Each feature is merged via a Pull Request (PR)
- At least one team member reviews a PR before merging
- One commit per student as required by the course

## Conflict Resolution

- Team members will communicate issues respectfully and directly
- If conflicts arise, the team will discuss them and reach consensus
- Unresolved issues will be escalated to the instructor if necessary

## Availability & Expectations

- Team members are expected to contribute regularly
- Any availability issues should be communicated early
- Deadlines will be respected to ensure timely submissions

## Agreement

By participating in this project, all team members agree to follow this charter and contribute to the success of the team.
